Our Story

Founded in 1995, Prakruthi Pre School began with a vision to create an institution that would redefine excellence in education. What started as a small school with 120 students has now grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Our founder, Dr. Ramesh Patel, believed that education should ignite curiosity and foster a lifelong love of learning. This philosophy continues to guide us as we innovate and adapt to meet the needs of today's students.

We take pride in our diverse community where students from all backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and prepare to make meaningful contributions to society.
