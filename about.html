Our Legacy of Educational Excellence

For over 28 years, Prakruthi Pre School has been shaping young minds and preparing leaders for tomorrow's challenges.

Our Mission & Vision

At Prakruthi Pre School, our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become compassionate, creative, and critical thinkers who will shape the future.

We envision a world where every student discovers their unique potential and develops the skills, knowledge, and character to make meaningful contributions to society.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, and Compassion guide everything we do.

Educational Philosophy

We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and character - through experiential learning.

Global Perspective

Our curriculum prepares students to be global citizens with cultural awareness and understanding.

Our History

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in education, our journey reflects our commitment to excellence.

1995

Foundation

Prakruthi Pre School was founded by Dr. Ramesh Patel with just 120 students and a vision to redefine education in the region.

2002

First Expansion

Added high school program and constructed our science wing, establishing our reputation for STEM excellence.

2010

Green Campus Initiative

Implemented solar panels and rainwater harvesting, becoming a model for sustainable school design.

2018

International Recognition

Awarded "Best Innovative School" by the International Education Society for our project-based curriculum.

2023

Present Day

Now serving over 2,500 students with state-of-the-art facilities and a 100% college acceptance rate.
