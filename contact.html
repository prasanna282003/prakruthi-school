Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school and admissions process.

What are the school hours? +

Our regular school hours are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM for all grades. Extended care options are available until 5:30 PM for an additional fee.

What is the admission process? +

The admission process includes submission of an application form, academic records review, student assessment (for certain grades), and a family interview. Please visit our Admissions page or contact our admissions office for detailed information.

What extracurricular activities are offered? +

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (cricket, basketball, swimming), music, dance, drama, robotics, debate, and various clubs. Activities vary by grade level and season.

What safety measures are in place? +

Student safety is our top priority. Our campus features 24/7 security, controlled access points, CCTV surveillance, regular safety drills, and a full-time nurse. All staff undergo thorough background checks.

Do you offer transportation services? +

Yes, we provide safe and reliable bus transportation covering most areas of the city. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking, seat belts, and trained attendants.
